## Função composta

$$g \circ f(x) = g(f(x))$$

**Exemplo 1**

Dadas $f(x) = 2x - 3$ e $g(x) = 4x + 1$, calcule:

- $g \circ f(x)$
$$g \circ f(x) = g(2x-3)$$
$$g \circ f(x) = 4(2x-3) + 1$$
$$g \circ f(x) = 8x-11$$

- $f \circ g(x)$
$$f \circ g(x) = f(4x+1)$$
$$f \circ g(x) = 2(4x+1) - 3$$
$$f \circ g(x) = 8x-1$$
